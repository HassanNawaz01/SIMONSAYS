Game guide

Practice

Train without connecting a wallet. Scores are not minted.

Onchain

Play solo, then mint your score on Base with a 0 ETH contract call plus gas.

Free 1v1

Match by wallet, both players press Ready, then a 5-second countdown starts the 60-second game.

Paid 1v1

Choose the same stake as your opponent. Both deposit and press Ready. A 0.5% fee is deducted from each stake only when a winner is settled.

Rewards

The winner receives the remaining combined stakes. Use Pending reward to withdraw winnings or recover an eligible refund.

Always verify the network, contract address, and ETH amount shown by your wallet before confirming.
